About and Rules:

This website consists of two geography games. First is a city guessing game where you are given the population, language, and the currency used in the city. You must guess the city you think it is and if correct you will gain a point. In the second game you must choose which city has a higher population. The goal of both games is to get the longest streak of correct answers that you can.
