About and Rules:

This website consists of two geography games. First is a city guessing game where you are given the population, language, life expectancy of the country the city is in, and the currency used in the city. You must guess the city you think it is, if correct you will gain a point. Your score will drop to zero and you will have to restart if incorrect. In the second game you must choose which city has a higher population. The goal of both games is to get the longest streak of correct answers that you can.
